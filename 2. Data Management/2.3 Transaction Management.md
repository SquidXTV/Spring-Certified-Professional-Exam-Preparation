### Isolation
- `READ_UNCOMMITED`
- `READ_COMITTED`
- `REPEATABLE_READ`
- `SERIALIZABLE`

### ACID
- Atomicity: single unit (all or nothing)
- Consistency: from valid state into a valid state
- Isolation: no interference between transactions
- Durability: commits (changes) are permanent, even after a crash
